Work

Little Thistle Floral Design is a home-based floral design studio specializing in natural and unique floral arrangements that are designed with love.

Weddings

I offer a complimentary consultation where we will walk through the floral design process together. I can help you choose a flower colour palette to best suit your wedding based on your venue, theme and décor choices. From start to finish, your wedding flower design process is a truly customized and personal experience.

Special event or corporate work also available upon request.

About

After completing my biology degree at Queen’s University, I knew I wanted to branch away from academia and spend some time tapping into my creative roots. Having loved arranging garden bouquets for my mom as a little girl, I decided to try my hand at floral design. I started my flower journey at Mood Moss Flowers in Ottawa, where I spent a year working and learning as an apprentice florist.

I discovered I was passionate about floral design and decided to pursue my own business when I moved to Halifax, Nova Scotia. You can now find me creating unique and beautiful arrangements in my home-based floral studio with my trusty sidekick Louie the English bulldog.
